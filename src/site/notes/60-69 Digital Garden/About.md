## *Notes on the margins, bad luck, and local history.*

***

I live and write in Pennsylvania. Most of my work deals with small-town folklore, humans being humans, and the complex nature of relationships.

When I'm not trying to fix a messy first draft, I spend my time digging through old records, keeping a meticulous commonplace book, and looking into things that are probably better left alone. 

### Current Fixations
- **Music:** Favorite band is Supertramp but I've been really into folk music lately.
- **Aesthetics:** Cryptids (mostly Mothman), weighted blankets, oversized hoodies, and crippling insomnia.
- **The Desk:** Messy as shit. I do not keep a clean work space.
